I make indie folk music for old souls.

My inspiration lies in the lake and pine of Northern Wisconsin, artists such as Novo Amor and Paul Simon, the wisdom of elders, and the consumption of many potatoes.

I also cover songs from all genres using a "folkified" one-man-band approach — anything from Michael Jackson, to Toto, to Owl City, to Red Hot Chili Peppers, to Gordon Lightfoot. Listen to a sample here.

Follow my most recent activity on Instagram or Facebook.

For bookings, collaborations and requests, shoot me an email. I do private events and wedding cocktail hours!
